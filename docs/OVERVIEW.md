# Business Overview & Site Map

## About the Business

**50inStyle** is a personal styling service run by **Limor Bachar**, an Israeli personal stylist based in Bat Hefer.
The brand is built around self-empowerment through fashion — helping women (primarily 50+) find their authentic style, build confidence, and fall in love with their wardrobe.

**Core philosophy:** Styling is not about fashion trends — it's self-expression. The right clothing conveys messages, builds confidence, and reflects the true self.
